These are the kinds of BATS that are used in the MLB

There are lots of types of bats in the Major Leagues. The two most people are familiar with are the standard bat and the torpedo bat.

The Standard

The standard baseball bat is made out of wood, most of the time the wood is oak or cedar. These bats are used by most players throughout the MLB and have been since the games creation. Some big companies that make bats are Louisville Slugger, Marucci, Victus, DeMarini and Rawlings.

The Torpedo bat

The torpedo bat is known for having a lot of pop and also producing lots of home runs. This bat is used by players such as Jazz Chisholm Jr,shown above, Cody Bellinger, Elly De La Cruz, also shown above, and Francisco Lindor. Some have tried to ban the bat saying that it has to much pop and is illegal but the comissioner has came out and said the bat is good for baseball, saying it still stays in the guidelines.
